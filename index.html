Ayoub Nouri is senior computer scientist and R&D Lab director. He has 15 years experience in scientific research in academia and industry in the field of software engineering and formal methods for distributed, embedded and intelligent systems. His experience span across basic research and development, research projects and roadmaps setup, transfer of academic research to industry, and research team management.

Dr. Nouri is leading the formal methods R&D Lab at Huawei France since April 2019. Before that, he held various research and teaching positions at CEA, University of Grenoble Alps and Verimag.
He obtained his PhD in computer science from the University of Grenoble Alps in 2015.

Dr. Nouri is interested in building innovative techniques and tools for complex and intelligent software system design and in applying them to solve concrete industrial problems in order to improve quality and productivity. His work focuses on formal model-based design, including formal modeling, formal verification, requirements specification and formalization, performance evaluation, machine learing, and automated code generation.
