Ayoub Nouri is a Senior Researcher at Huawei France since April 2019. He got his PhD in computer science from University of Grenoble Alpes in 2015 and a Master of research in Software and Critical Systems in 2011. He held reseache positions in CEA and Verimag Lab.

Dr. Nouri is interested in new methods for system design to achieve constructivity and productivity. He focuses on model-based and component-based approaches using formal techniques, Performance Evaluation, Automatic code generation and Machine Learning.
