Ayoub Nouri is a Senior Researcher at Huawei since April 2019.
He obtained a PhD. in computer science from University of Grenoble Alpes in 2015 and a Master of research in Software and Critical Systems in 2011. He did two postdocs in CEA and Verimag respectively in 2015 and 2016. Dr. Nouri is interested in new methods for system design to achieve constructivity and productivity. He contributed among others to Model-based and Component-based approaches using formal techniques, Performance Evaluation, Automatic code generation and Machine Learning.
